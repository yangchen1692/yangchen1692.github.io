I am a Ph.D. student in the [Mary Frances Early College of Education](https://coe.uga.edu/) at [The University of Georgia (UGA)](https://www.uga.edu/), advised by [Dr. Gayle Andrews](https://people.coe.uga.edu/gayle-andrews/).

My research focuses on the construction and negotiation of teachers' professional identities: how teachers come to understand and navigate who they are as educators across their classrooms, schools, and broader professional communities.
